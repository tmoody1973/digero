Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a recipe from a cookbook?

Tap the "Scan" button on the home screen, point your camera at the cookbook page, and take a photo. Digero will automatically extract the recipe text. Review and edit the extracted content, then save to your collection.

How do I import a recipe from a website?

Tap "Add Recipe" and select "From URL". Paste the recipe website link and Digero will automatically extract the recipe details.

How do I import a recipe from YouTube?

Paste a YouTube video URL in the "From URL" import, or browse the Discover tab to find cooking channels. Tap "Save Recipe" on any video to extract the recipe.

What are the free tier limits?

Free users can save up to 10 recipes and perform 3 cookbook scans per month. Upgrade to Digero Pro for unlimited recipes and scans.

How do I generate a shopping list?

Add recipes to your meal plan, then go to the Shopping List tab. Tap "Generate from Meal Plan" to create an organized grocery list with combined ingredients.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Subscription and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous Digero Pro subscription linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Subscription and tap "Manage Subscription" to be taken to your Apple subscription settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently delete your account and all associated data.
