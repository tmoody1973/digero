Digero Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Digero ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Profile picture (if using social sign-in)

Recipe Data

We store the recipes you save, including:

  • Recipe titles, ingredients, and instructions
  • Images you upload or scan
  • Source URLs and video references
  • Your cookbooks and meal plans
  • Shopping lists

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • App usage patterns (features used, session duration)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Crash reports and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Digero service
  • Process your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists
  • Manage your subscription and payments
  • Send important service updates
  • Improve our app and develop new features

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Clerk - Authentication and user management
  • Convex - Database and backend services
  • RevenueCat - Subscription and payment processing
  • Google Gemini API - Recipe extraction from images
  • YouTube Data API - Video metadata for recipe import

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use:

  • HTTPS for all data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Secure authentication tokens

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your recipes and data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Digero is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@digero.app
